About us

Born on the Peninsula.
Built around the table.

Two parents, a love for hosting, and a Covid-era obsession with dough. We got tired of the same delivery boxes and wanted pizza to be something people actually gather around. So we brought the oven to you.

The name? Firen (pronounced fi-rren) means oven in Arabic. The kids heard Fire N Pizza. It stuck. Both feel right.
