About us

Born on the Peninsula.
Built around the table.

Firen Pizza started with a simple idea — bring people together around the joy of making and eating great pizza. Whether it's a birthday for ten kids or a corporate team of fifty, we show up with everything you need for an experience people actually talk about.

We're a mobile operation, which means we come to you — backyard, office, park, or venue. Our oven does the heavy lifting. You just bring the people.
